About Us

Welcome to TiffinBites, your go-to destination for authentic South Indian flavors in the heart of Hyderabad! We serve delicious home-style meals made fresh every day.

Our Story: Born out of a love for traditional food, TiffinBites began as a small family-run venture aiming to bring homely and hygienic tiffin to busy working professionals and students.

Timings: 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM (All days)

Location: Chaitanyapuri, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
